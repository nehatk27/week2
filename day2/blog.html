Art for Act

January 31, 2026

So, I was able to find an eclectic gallery nearby that accepts a wide variety of art from local artists. I decided it was time to market my mini prints in this very special Downtown Fort Myers space.

Flash forward, it has been a few months since I introduced my artwork in this format. Customers and other artists seem to be taking an interest in my style and the high-quality prints that I offer. All good for making conversations and new friends, not to mention the uptick in sales from this new-to-me venue.

Art for ACT is a not-so-typical gallery, as it includes lots of handmade items, but also: donated designer clothing, purses and accessories, even vintage and boho decor. The organizartion ACT actually serves a great cause, as it directly benefits recovering victims of domestic violence and trafficking.

The renovated century-old building still holds lots of squeaky character and brick charm.

It’s been fun meeting and greeting the tourists and locals who enjoy exploring the hidden gems of historical Downtown Fort Myers.

Traveling the World on a Budget

January 10, 2026

Traveling the world on a budget is possible with some careful planning and smart choices. Here are some tips to help you explore the world without breaking the bank:

  • Plan Ahead: Research your destination and plan your trip in advance to find the best deals on flights, accommodations, and activities.
  • Travel During Off-Peak Seasons: Avoid traveling during peak tourist seasons to save money on flights and accommodations.
  • Use Budget Airlines: Consider flying with budget airlines that offer lower fares compared to traditional carriers.
  • Stay in Hostels or Budget Accommodations: Opt for hostels, guesthouses, or budget hotels instead of luxury accommodations to save money on lodging.
  • Cook Your Own Meals: Save money on food by cooking your own meals or eating at local markets and street food vendors.
  • Use Public Transportation: Utilize public transportation options like buses, trains, or subways to get around instead of taxis or rental cars.
  • Take Advantage of Free Attractions: Look for free attractions, museums, parks, and events in your destination to enjoy without spending money.
  • Travel Light: Avoid checked baggage fees by packing light and using a carry-on bag for your travels.
